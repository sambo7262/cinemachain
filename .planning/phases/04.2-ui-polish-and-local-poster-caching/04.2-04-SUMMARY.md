## Decisions Made
- `willChange: transform` on column div, not img — GPU compositing targets the animated element
- `blur-[20px]` on column container, not per-image — avoids per-image GPU compositing (NAS optimization per UI-SPEC.md)
- `distributeColumns` round-robin across 4 columns — even distribution regardless of poster count, no empty-column edge cases
- `posterUrl` prepends `/api` to `poster_local_path` — nginx proxies `/api/static/posters/` to backend `/static/posters/`; TMDB w185 CDN fallback when null
