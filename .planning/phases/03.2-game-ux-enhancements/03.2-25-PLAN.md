<objective>
Fix the regression introduced in 03.2-20: `_ensure_movie_cast_in_db` silently skips all cast members for CSV-imported sessions because the Movie record doesn't exist in the `movies` table at the time of the on-demand fallback call.

Two surgical fixes in `backend/app/routers/game.py`:
- Fix A: Upsert a minimal Movie stub before the cast loop, then resolve the Movie PK once outside the loop (eliminates 20 redundant SELECTs and the FK failure).
- Fix B: Add `BackgroundTasks` to `import_csv_session` and call `_prefetch_credits_background(last_movie_id, tmdb)` after the WatchEvent commit, mirroring `create_session`.

Purpose: After CSV import, eligible actors and movies must work on the first request. Without these fixes the game is completely broken after any CSV import.

Output: Modified `backend/app/routers/game.py`. No other files changed.
</objective>
